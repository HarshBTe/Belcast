Vision, Mission & About Us

🚀 Vision

Belcast Industries strives to spark innovation by serving as a hub for creative ideas and effective problem-solving. This includes advancing breakthrough technologies, nurturing a culture of ongoing improvement, and partnering with others to push industry progress forward.

🎯 Mission

At Belcast, our mission is to shape the future of manufacturing by delivering high-quality cast metal components with precision, reliability, and innovation. We support a wide range of industries through advanced foundry practices, sustainable production methods, and continuous technological advancement. By fostering strong partnerships and a culture of craftsmanship, we aim to be a trusted cornerstone in the global supply chain.

— ABOUT US —

Engineering Excellence in Every Casting

Belcast Industries, a subsidiary of Ductile Iron Works, was established in 2001 under visionary leadership. Today, we are a trusted manufacturer of high-quality castings serving industries like Automobile, Power, Oil & Gas, Marine, and Defense.

With advanced foundry practices and a commitment to precision, we deliver reliable solutions that power critical industrial applications worldwide.

Quality Control

Ensuring precision, consistency, and excellence in every casting

Raw Material Inspection

All incoming raw materials are tested for chemical composition and quality compliance. We ensure only certified materials are used in production to maintain consistency and strength.

Melting & Pouring Control

Strict monitoring of furnace temperature, composition, and pouring parameters ensures defect-free casting and optimal metallurgical properties.

In-Process Inspection

Continuous inspection during molding, machining, and finishing stages ensures dimensional accuracy and adherence to client specifications.

Testing & Measurement

Equipped with advanced tools like CMM, hardness testers, and surface finish testers to ensure every product meets strict quality standards.

Final Inspection & Dispatch

Each product undergoes final inspection before dispatch to ensure zero defects and complete customer satisfaction.
